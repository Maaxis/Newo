Set the Board object's time_setting variable according to the board's Date Format in the Admin CP.

For now, Display Seconds must always be ON and Relative Time must always be OFF for any time-parsing related functions to work.

1 = 13th Aug 2022 5:34:01 PM
2 = Saturday 13th August 2022, 5:34:01 PM
3 = 13/08/2022 5:34:01 PM
4 = 13/08/2022 17:34:01
5 = 08/13/2022 5:34:01 PM
6 = 08/13/2022 17:34:01
